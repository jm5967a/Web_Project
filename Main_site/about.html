About Us



Here at Discover DC we want to enhance your DC experience by connecting you with other people in the area.

We wanted to create one site with all of DC's top tourist places as well as those hidden gems only locals know. You can share your experiences and post reviews for every place in DC that you visit.

Think we are missing some of DC's most important places? Let us know your favorite restaurants in DC and post it to our Restaurants page!
